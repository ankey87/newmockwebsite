Meet Amanda

Need a passionate UX/UI Engineer to help bring more creativity and diversity to your client projects? Meet Amanda Key, she is a 32 year old mother of two who decided to leave her career in teaching to pursue a new dream. In July 2019 she began the UX/UI Engineering program at Kenzie University. In January 2020 she received her UX Design certificate, and is now working towards completing the UIE portion of the program.

When Amanda is not working on her certification, she loves working at her job with Southern Legacy Marketing & Design, and spending time with her two beautiful children. She enjoys reading and listening to music, but her passion is design and development. Ready to see more?
